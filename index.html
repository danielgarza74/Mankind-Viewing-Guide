The Story of All of Us

MANKIND

A 12-part historical documentary series chronicling the history of humanity from the dawn of civilization to the modern era.

Episode 01

Inventors

Traces the dawn of mankind, from mastering fire and the Agricultural Revolution to the construction of the first cities and pyramids, alongside the birth of early warfare.

Episode 02

Iron Men

Examines the collapse of the Bronze Age empires, the democratization of power through the discovery of iron, and the emergence of early democracy in Athens.

Episode 03

Empires

Focuses on the expansive trade networks and infrastructure of the Roman Empire, which inadvertently facilitated the rapid, global spread of Christianity.

Episode 04

Warriors

Explores the power vacuums left by the fall of Rome, covering the rapid expansion of Islam, the Viking invasions, and the Crusades.

Episode 05

Plague

Details the brutal conquests of Genghis Khan, which created the largest contiguous land empire in history, and the devastating sweep of the bubonic plague.

Episode 06

Survivors

Highlights the European rebirth fueled by African gold, the rise of Venice as a financial hub, and the transformative power of firearms in unifying China.

Episode 07

New World

Follows the Vikings' journey to the Americas, the rise of the sophisticated Aztec civilization, and the eventual collision of the Old and New Worlds following Columbus's voyages.

Episode 08

Treasure

Centers on the massive silver strikes in the Americas that birthed true global trade, tracking the resulting economic booms and the severe human cost of this new system.

Episode 09

Pioneers

Covers the age of navigation, the early colonization of North America, the lucrative global fur trade, and the dawn of the Scientific Revolution.

Episode 10

Revolutions

Focuses on the fight for liberty during the American Revolution and the massive societal and economic shifts driven by the Industrial Revolution.

Episode 11

Speed

Examines the post-Civil War era defined by mass production, Japan's incredibly rapid modernization, and the disasters that occurred when progress outpaced safety.

Episode 12

New Frontiers

Concludes the series in the modern age, looking at agricultural breakthroughs, the discovery of penicillin, and humanity's continued drive to explore and innovate.
